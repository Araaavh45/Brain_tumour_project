About BrainCare System

Advanced AI-powered brain tumor detection with comprehensive patient support

Our Mission

To democratize access to advanced brain tumor detection technology and create a supportive community that connects patients, donors, and healthcare professionals for comprehensive care.

We believe that early detection saves lives, and no one should face a brain tumor diagnosis alone. Our platform combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with human compassion to provide both medical screening and emotional support.

Our Vision

A world where brain tumor detection is accessible, accurate, and supported by a caring community of healthcare professionals and volunteers.

We envision a future where AI-assisted medical screening is seamlessly integrated with human support networks, ensuring that every patient receives not just a diagnosis, but also the care and support they need throughout their journey.

Our Technology

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)

Our primary detection model uses deep learning to analyze MRI brain scans with 85% accuracy, identifying potential tumor presence.

K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)

Secondary classification system that determines tumor types (Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary) based on feature similarity matching.

Feature Extraction

Advanced feature extraction techniques that analyze image patterns and match them against our comprehensive tumor database.
